# intel-sustainability-dashboard

## Objective
Analyze Intel’s energy and water usage across the United States over a one-year period, examining production, supply and demand, renewable energy, utility power sources, hourly generation differences, and regional energy sources to provide actionable insights for sustainability and resource optimization.

---

## Data
- Publicly available Intel sustainability datasets for a single year  
- Metrics include:
  - Energy production, supply, and demand by region  
  - Renewable energy usage  
  - Utility power sources  
  - Hourly differences in energy generation  
  - Energy sources by region  
- Coverage across multiple operational sites in the U.S.

---

## Tools
- **Tableau**: Interactive dashboards and visualizations  

---

## Dashboard Overview
The interactive dashboard provides a detailed view of Intel’s energy and water usage over the course of one year. Users can explore patterns by region, energy source, and hour. Key visualizations include:  
- Regional energy production vs. demand  
- Renewable energy contribution by site and region  
- Hourly generation differences  
- Energy source breakdown by region  
- Filters for region, energy source, and site for detailed analysis  

---

## Key Insights
- Regional differences in energy production and renewable adoption are significant  
- Hourly generation data shows peaks and gaps in supply vs. demand, highlighting opportunities for efficiency improvements  
- Utility power sources vary by region, presenting areas for optimization  
- One-year trends provide a clear snapshot of Intel’s sustainability performance  

---

## Business Impact
- Supports data-driven decisions for sustainability and resource management  
- Identifies opportunities to increase renewable energy use and balance supply and demand  
- Communicates performance and optimization opportunities to leadership  
- Highlights actionable areas for improving energy efficiency and water conservation  

---
## Deliverables
- Interactive Tableau dashboard  
- Publicly available datasets for the one-year analysis  
- Summary of key insights and findings
